# Skyblock Rules

These rules apply specifically to the Skyblock gamemode and are enforced alongside the [Global Rules](/rules).

## Boosting

Players and teams may not boost others in ways that create an unfair competitive advantage. Boosting for seasonal
rewards is not allowed and may disqualify all involved players from rewards.

## Alt Accounts

Alt accounts are allowed with restrictions:

- Do not use alts for automation, macros, or auto clickers.
- Do not use alts to exploit mechanics or duplication bugs.
- Do not use alts to grind skills for extra rewards.
- All of your accounts must be on the same Skyblock island.
- Maximum of 5 alt accounts.
- Multiple accounts may vote and claim vote rewards unless this policy changes.

## AFK Clicking and Macros

AFK auto clicking is allowed. Macros and scripted action chains are not allowed.
