# Workbench
The Workbench is the main way to craft and unlock new machines within Skyblock.
It can be purchased in `/shop` under the Miscellaneous section.

![img.png](/image/md/wiki/skyblock/workbench/workbenchmodel.png)

When opening the Workbench, you’ll find different categories based on item types, including:

- Power
- Water
- Parts
- Robots
- TNT Spawners
- Item Management

![img.png](/image/md/wiki/skyblock/workbench/workbench.png)

#### How Crafting Works:
Click on an item to view its recipe.
Curious how to craft an ingredient? You can click on an ingredient to view its crafting recipe.

#### Once you have everything:

Click the item on the right side to craft it.
If successful, the item will be added to your inventory.
If you are missing materials, red blinking blocks will appear on the left hand side.

The Workbench allows you to progressively craft stronger machines and expand your island’s capabilities.
