# Workbench

The Workbench is used to craft and unlock many of the custom machines available in Skyblock. It can be purchased from
`/shop` under the Miscellaneous section.

![img.png](/image/md/wiki/skyblock/workbench/workbenchmodel.png)

When opening the Workbench, items are separated into different categories based on what they are used for. These
categories include:

* Power
* Water
* Parts
* Robots
* TNT Spawners
* Item Management
* Island Value

![img.png](/image/md/wiki/skyblock/workbench/workbench.png)

#### How Crafting Works

Click an item inside the Workbench to view its crafting recipe. If one of the required ingredients is also crafted in
the Workbench, you can click that ingredient to view its recipe as well.

This allows you to check what materials are needed for each part of a recipe without leaving the Workbench menu.

#### Crafting an Item

Once you have all of the required materials, click the item on the right side of the menu to craft it. If the craft is
successful, the item will be added to your inventory.

If you are missing any required materials, red blinking blocks will appear on the left side of the menu. These blocks
show which materials are missing from the recipe.

The Workbench is important for progressing your island, as it allows you to craft stronger machines, higher-tier items,
and more advanced systems over time.
