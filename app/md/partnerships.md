## 🤝 **Partner Information**

Regarding partnerships at **Static**, we have a set of rules and requirements outlined below.

---

🌟 **VIP Partnership Requirements**

To qualify for a **VIP Partnership**, your server must meet the following:

* **Minimum of 1,500 members**
* **Strictly anti-NSFW content**
* **Promotional pings are negotiable**
* **Partnerships are featured in our VIP partnership channel**

---

🔹 **Base / Standard Partnership Requirements**

To qualify for a **Base Partnership**, your server must meet the following:

* **Minimum of 150 members**
* **Strictly anti-NSFW content**
* **Promotional pings are negotiable but not guaranteed**
* **Partnerships are posted in our standard partnership channel**

---

## ❓ **Questions & Answers**

**Q:** What’s the difference between VIP and Base partnerships?

**A:** The main differences are visibility and promotional benefits.

* **VIP Partnerships** are featured in a dedicated VIP channel and are **more likely to receive a promotional ping
  ** (subject to negotiation).
* **Base Partnerships** are placed in the standard partnership channel and have a **lower chance of receiving a ping
  **.

---

**Q:** If I meet both VIP and Base requirements, can I choose both?

**A:** No. If you meet both requirements, you must **choose one partnership tier**:

* Either **VIP** *or* **Base**
* You **cannot combine benefits** from both tiers

---

**Q:** What is a partnership and how does it work?

**A:** A partnership is a **mutual agreement between Discord servers** to help grow their communities. The process is
simple:

* **Open a support ticket** in our Discord
* **Provide your server invite link**
* We **exchange partnership links**
* Optional: **Discuss promotional pings** within the ticket

---
