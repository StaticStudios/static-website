# Global Rules

These are the network-wide rules for Static Studios. They apply across all servers, game modes, and community spaces,
including in-game chat and Discord.

Rules may be updated at any time. It is your responsibility to stay up to date.

## Respectful Behavior

Be respectful in all communication. Harassment, hate speech, excessive toxicity, threats, and spam are not allowed.
Impersonating staff members, posting NSFW content, or sharing unsafe links is also prohibited.

## Fair Play

Any cheat, exploit, or unfair advantage is not allowed. This includes hacked clients, automation, and intentional abuse
of bugs or unintended mechanics.

If you discover a bug or exploit, report it to staff immediately.

## Real-World Trading

Trading in-game items, currencies, or services for real-world money, goods, or external services is prohibited.

## Staff Authority

Staff decisions on rule interpretation and enforcement are final. Punishments may still be issued for harmful behavior
that is not explicitly listed.

## Language Policy

Use English in public communication channels to ensure moderation and support remain effective.

## Scamming

Scamming, insiding, or deceptive behavior to gain items, currency, or advantages from other players is not allowed.

## Common Sense and Enforcement

Staff may act on harmful behavior even when not explicitly listed in these rules.
